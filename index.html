About Us

Establishing Trust Since 2000

Located in the heart of Thoothukudi (Tuticorin), Tamil Nadu, Ganesh Salt Traders has grown to become a premier manufacturer, trader, and wholesaler of high-quality salt products.

With over two decades of experience, we are committed to delivering purity and excellence. Our state-of-the-art processing ensures that every grain of salt meets the highest standards of quality and hygiene. We serve a diverse clientele across India, offering tailored solutions for both domestic and industrial needs.
